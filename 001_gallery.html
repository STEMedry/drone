Introduction to Drone Technology

Drone technology represents a revolutionary engineering field that integrates aerodynamics, electronics, computer science, and innovative design. Dynamic Remotely Operated Navigation Equipments (Drones), or Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), comprise aircraft systems capable of remote control or autonomous operation. Their applications extend across cinematography, education, agriculture, and space exploration. Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) technology serves as a fundamental component in drone positioning, enabling precise navigation and location tracking that enhances operational effectiveness across diverse applications.
