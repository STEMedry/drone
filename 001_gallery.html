Drone Technology: Exploring the Future of Flight

Introduction to Drone Technology

Drone technology is a groundbreaking area of engineering that merges aerodynamics, electronics, computer science, and innovative design. Dynamic Remotely Operated Navigation Equipments (Drones), also known as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), are aircraft that can be controlled remotely or operate autonomously. Their applications range from cinematography and education to agriculture and space exploration.
