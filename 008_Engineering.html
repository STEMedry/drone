Drone-assisted Water Sampling System

System Components

  • Quadcopter drone
  • 2-meter-long string
  • Inverted plastic bottle
  • Iron ball

Water Sampling Process

  • Drone carries bottle sealed by iron ball
  • Descends to water surface
  • Iron ball sinks, opening bottle to collect water
  • Ascends, pulling string to reseal bottle
  • Flies to collection bucket
  • Descends to bucket with "O" ring
  • Releases iron ball to discharge water

Water Sampling Principle

無人機輔助水樣採集系統

系統組件

  • 四旋翼無人機
  • 2米長繩索
  • 倒置塑料瓶
  • 鐵球

水樣採集過程

  • 無人機攜帶被鐵球密封的瓶子
  • 下降至水面
  • 鐵球下沉，開啟瓶口收集水樣
  • 上升，拉動繩索重新關閉瓶口
  • 飛往收集桶
  • 下降至帶有"O"型環的收集桶
  • 垂下鐵球排出水樣
