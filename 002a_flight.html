Welcome Pilot!

You are on a hoop-run mission. Take off, pass every hoop in order, and bring your drone home safely to win.

Read the Arena

Think in three directions while flying: left-right, forward-backward, and up-down. Smooth control in all three is the key to clean runs.

Core Moves You’ll Use

Master these moves and you’ll clear hoops faster and safer:

  • Throttle Up Climb to match hoop height.
  • Throttle Down Descend for low lines and landing.
  • Yaw Left Turn left to line up your next target.
  • Yaw Right Turn right to set your approach.
  • Pitch Forward Drive forward through the course.
  • Pitch Backward Slow and pull back to recover spacing.
  • Roll Left Slide left for side-to-side correction.
  • Roll Right Slide right to center in the hoop.

How to Play

Use touch joysticks or keyboard—whatever feels best for your flying style:

  • Left Joystick: Altitude and turning.
  • Right Joystick: Forward/back and side movement.
  • Keyboard Controls:
    • W/S: Throttle Up/Down
    • A/D: Yaw Left/Right
    • Arrow Up/Down: Pitch Forward/Backward
    • Arrow Left/Right: Roll Left/Right
    • Space: Toggle Advanced mode.
    • F: Toggle FPV view.
    • H: Start Return-to-Home (RTH).
    • R: Reset mission.
  • DEMO Button: Auto-fly all hoops and land at home. You can turn it off anytime and continue manually.

Mission Goal

Pass all hoops in sequence. Keep your line clean, avoid edge contact, then return and land at the home marker.

  • You will get encouragement pop-ups after 3 hoops and 6 hoops.

Modes & Feedback

  • Advanced mode adds dynamic hoop behavior and challenge.
  • If you hit hoop edges badly, warning audio triggers right away.
  • Crashed hoop numbers turn red in Hoop Status.
  • Your crash total is tracked in the stats panel.
  • Use FPV mode when you want an immersive pilot view.
  • DEMO works together with Advanced and/or FPV mode.

Safety Net: RTH

If things get messy, activate RTH and let the drone come back automatically.

  • During RTH, nearby hoops are auto-detected and bypassed to reduce crash risk.
  • Completion congratulations always match your selected language.

Tips to Improve Your Score

  • Approach hoops from centerline, not at sharp angles.
  • Use small corrections instead of sudden full-stick moves.
  • Control speed before each hoop, then accelerate out.
  • Review the top-view replay to learn better routes.

Ready to Fly

Take off, stay smooth, clear every hoop, and land like a pro. Good luck, pilot!
