Drone-assisted Water Sampling System

System Components

  • Quadcopter drone
  • 2-meter-long string
  • Inverted plastic bottle
  • Iron ball

Water Sampling Process

Water Retrieval

  • Drone carries sampling bottle sealed with iron ball
  • Descends to target water surface
  • Iron ball sinks upon contact, allowing water collection
  • Ascends while pulling string to reseal bottle

Water Discharge

  • Flies to discharge station
  • Aligns with collection bucket guided by "O" ring
  • Releases iron ball to discharge water sample

Drone-assisted Water Sampling Protocol

無人機輔助水樣採集系統

系統組件

  • 四旋翼無人機
  • 2米長繩索
  • 倒置塑料瓶
  • 鐵球

水樣採集過程

取水過程

  • 無人機攜帶鐵球密封的採樣瓶
  • 下降至目標水面
  • 鐵球接觸水面後下沉，開啟瓶口收集水樣
  • 上升時拉動繩索重新密封瓶口

排水過程

  • 飛往排放站
  • 對準帶有"O"型環的收集桶
  • 釋放鐵球排出水樣
